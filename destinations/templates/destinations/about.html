About Me

I am Jeetraj Shah from Mumbai, India. In my career I have tried many things like Business Analysis, Project Management and a bit of Product Management. My interests include Travel, eCommerce and Technology which has made me go on this incredible journey.

About Travel Slow

Travel Slow is a project close to my heart, that I was always thinking, planning or ranting about. But finally I decided to build it as my final submission for the Harvard CS50's Web Programming Course. This is a concept in travel that helps users to plan their travel to places which match their actual dreams or wishes rather than blindly follow whatever is pushed to them. People rarely know what is available out there in world. So a city or destination search using name of the destination is rarely helpful. Also we see that majority of the travel destinations are trend based. Hence the problems of bad experiences for the visitors, hosts and people involved in the business.

We aim to provide a unique set of destinations that are not too crowded, meet traveller expectations and are beautiful at the same time.

The Future

I will keep building this project further, in a worst case scenario - as a one man company. My future plans include (but not limited to):

  • Improve the AI Model further and reduce dependency on Open AI API
  • Integration with Hotel or Apartment Suppliers
  • Integration with Flights
  • Integration with Ground Transport (Self Drive, Transfers and other options)
  • Integration with Activity Suppliers
